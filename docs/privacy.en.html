Privacy Policy

We protect your privacy and deliver a safe experience.

What we store on device

  • VirusTotal API key: stored securely using SecureStore.
  • General settings (HTTPS warning, URL scanning, language, theme): local only.
  • Scan history: remains on your device.

When URLs are sent

When "URL scanning" is enabled, the URL may be sent to VirusTotal for security analysis per their privacy policy.

No Internet

If offline, nothing is sent to VirusTotal. The app will pass the URL directly to you according to settings.

Personal data

The app does not collect personal data or analyze content beyond URLs you choose to scan.
